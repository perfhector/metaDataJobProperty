import info.perfhector.jenkinsci.plugins.metadatajobproperty.MetadataJobProperty

Be aware that accessing to currentJob depends on the groovy plugin, you're using: * currentJob in case of EnvInject plugin * build.project in case of Scriptler * build.project in case of Groovy Postbuild * ...
